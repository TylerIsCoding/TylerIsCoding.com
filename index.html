My Mission

Do you have a web presence?

If you or someone you know needs to show a client what you can provide, how will you show them? How will your website draw them in? What happens if they look at your site on a phone? A tablet?

It is crucial that the websites that represent us and our business are responsive and accessible. Responsive design allows your website content to flow freely across all screen resolutions and sizes, and renders it to look great on all devices. It also makes it unnecessary to maintain different versions of your website for mobile and desktop and saves you time, resources and efforts.

Accessibility means that all people can perceive, understand, navigate, and interact with electronic information and be active, contributing members of the digital world. Visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, and neurological disabilities should be taken into account when implementing accessibility measures.

Services

My Services

I dedicate myself to meet and exceed my clients initial vision of their passion project. Knowing what to expect when we work together is critical - here is how I deliver success:

Consultation

We will discuss your project with a problem-solving attitude to suggest, recommend, and design the best solution for your project while I focus on writing the code that meets the project's specifications and your needs.

Planning

After we decide what you want your dream project to be, I will begin the planning phase. I will draw up a proposal for you that includes a visual preview, a plan of action, and a creation timeline.

Creation

Once we come to an agreement with the proposal, I will begin to create your project. I will give frequent updates of progress made along the way. Communication is key at this stage and I will make sure that you are aware of any hurdles and milestones as I create and test your project.

Launch

Once I have completed the project that represents your final vision, we will go over your project one last time. If everything is up to both of our standards we will launch your project and I will provide ongoing support in accordance to our initial agreement.
