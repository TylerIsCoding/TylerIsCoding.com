Meet the team


Meet the beauticians and stylists behind our doors

Our Team

Spectral Salon is a new way of caring for your hair, skin, and body. Our salon specializes in color treatments and we employ only the best colorists and stylists with the top training. Opened in 2007, we have been providing a fun, relaxed, and professional atmosphere for our growing client base.

Ayesha Cole

Ayesha is a master stylist as well as salon owner. She graduated from the cosmetology program at Empire Beauty School in Malden in 1994. She has been in the salon industry for 25+ years. She loves the opportunity to make someone look great by either changing color, adding a few highlights, or just giving a new haircut. Ayesha also attends regular training classes to keep up with the latest trends.

Sarah Bellapont

Sarah has worked at Spectral Salon for 8 years. She graduated from Rob Roy Academy in Worcester and continues to educate herself on all the new color/cutting trends and techniques. She strives to have every client leave her chair feeling confident and beautiful. She specializes in color, vivid colors, blonding, balayage, Brazilian Blowout, tape-in extensions and updos.

Judy Albertson

Judy has been doing hair since 2005. She has traveled to Germany & Los Angeles to attend Goldwell's International Color Zoom and continuous, ongoing education. She leads our education program for apprentices and teaches many classes. She is a specialist in double process blonde and corrective color. She is also certified in 4 different hair extension methods and has a large following for her eyebrow shaping.
