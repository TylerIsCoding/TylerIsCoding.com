Restaurant Story

Paterro's was founded by world star chef Anthony Paterro in 2012 with his wife Angelica. Since 2012, they have expanded their restaurant into two more locations. One in Chicago and one in New York City. This Italian-Mediterranean fusion restaurant was the talk of Boston and has since been featured in the Improper Bostonian, Phantom Gourmet, and many other publications.


If you're in the mood for some serious flavor and healthy, delicious, Italian-Mediterranean cuisine; please make a reservation below as we do book fast. Thanks for visiting.
