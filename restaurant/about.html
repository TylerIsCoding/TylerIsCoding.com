Our Story

Paterro's Kitchen is a family-owned Italian restaurant that has been serving authentic Italian cuisine for over 10 years. The restaurant was founded by Anthony Paterro, who is a passionate and experienced chef with a deep love for Italian food.

Anthony grew up in an Italian-American family and was exposed to the traditional flavors of Italy from a young age. He began his culinary journey working in various Italian restaurants before opening his own restaurant. He spent years perfecting his craft, learning traditional techniques and experimenting with new flavors.

In the year 2013, Anthony opened the doors of Paterro's Kitchen, with a vision of providing customers with a true taste of Italy. He wanted to create a warm and inviting space where people could come together to enjoy delicious food, good wine, and great company.

For the past 10 years, Anthony has been dedicated to providing his customers with the best possible dining experience. He sources the freshest ingredients and prepares them with care and attention to detail. His menu features a wide variety of traditional Italian dishes, made with authentic recipes passed down through generations.

Today, Paterro's Kitchen has become a beloved destination for Italian food lovers, known for its delicious food, friendly service, and cozy atmosphere. Anthony and his team are dedicated to preserving the traditions of Italian cuisine and providing a true taste of Italy to every customer who walks through the door.
